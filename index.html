What is Bitcoin BSC?

BITCOIN BSC is a green BEP-20 version of Bitcoin built on BNB Smart Chain, simulated to start at an early point in Bitcoin’s history in 2011. Just as Bitcoin releases new BTC every 10 minutes, our staking smart contract pays out BTCBSC tokens in line with this block schedule. Stake BTCBSC to earn these rewards.
